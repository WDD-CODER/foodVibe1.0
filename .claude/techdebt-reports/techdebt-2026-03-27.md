### Style Violations

#### `as any` Type Casts — URL Param Serializer (5 files, carry-over)
All are the same pattern: `StringParam as any`, `StringSetParam as any`, `NullableStringParam as any`. Root cause: `useQueryParams()` serializer typing is not generic enough for custom serializers.

**Fix:** Create a `TypedUrlParamSerializer<T>` wrapper type or cast at the `list-state` utility level once.

| `pages/venues/components/venue-list/venue-list.component.ts` | 93 | `StringSetParam as any` |
